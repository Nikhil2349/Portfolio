The Credit Card Fraud Detection project is designed to identify and prevent fraudulent credit card transactions using machine learning techniques. Fraudulent transactions pose a significant risk to financial institutions and cardholders, and detecting these anomalies is crucial for minimizing financial loss and ensuring the security of credit card systems.

Blinkit Market Sales Analysis

The Blinkit Sales Analysis in Power BI provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance across various categories . Key metrics such as total sales, average sales, no.of items and average rating have been visualized to identify trends and growth opportunities. The analysis highlights peak sales periods, top-performing products, and customer buying behavior. Interactive dashboards allow stakeholders to drill down into specific data points, facilitating informed decision-making.
